About Us

Medi-Call is a simple web application to make your medical appointment from wherever you are. The only two things that you need are: an account and internet connection. That's all you need!

We hope this web application can help patients save time when scheduling an appointment. And that doctors can have a better control over the people who will attend each day. We hope it helps you!
